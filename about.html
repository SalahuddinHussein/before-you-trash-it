About

This site came about while I was moving. Packing is a big process, and I was trying to simplify things by getting rid of as much as possible. I'm a pretty environmentally-conscious person, and I try to throw away as little trash as possible. This got me thinking that I should make it as easy as possible to figure out what you can bring to donate, and where to bring it.

So please, use this tool and share with your friends. We live in a dying world - it's our responsibility to divert as much as we can away from landfills. When someone says "throw it away", just remember - there is no "away".
